About Me

My name is Sam Trouy, and I am a full stack developer in Atlanta, GA. I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving and creating unique solutions. I have experience with different languages, though the longer I develop the more I've realized the importance of creative problem-solving over knowing the specifics of a language or framework. I think this has helped me to adapt to new things quickly. I am not a designer by any means, but I do enjoy both backend and frontend development. Problems that are some mix of programming and math often catch my interest the most. I do my best to keep up with new frameworks, but I also appreciate making things as simple as possible.
